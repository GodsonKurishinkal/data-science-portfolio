üìã Project Overview

The Dynamic Pricing Engine is a comprehensive machine learning system that optimizes product pricing using advanced econometric analysis, demand forecasting, and mathematical optimization techniques. Built for retail and e-commerce applications, it maximizes revenue while considering market dynamics, competitive positioning, and inventory constraints.

This project demonstrates end-to-end data science capabilities: from econometric modeling (price elasticity analysis) to predictive ML (demand forecasting) to operations research (price optimization). It's designed as a production-ready system with comprehensive testing, documentation, and deployment considerations.

‚ú® Key Features

  • Price Elasticity Analysis: Calculate own-price and cross-price elasticities using log-log regression, arc elasticity, and point elasticity methods. Segment products by demand sensitivity (elastic/inelastic).
  • Demand Forecasting: ML-powered forecasting using XGBoost with 50+ engineered features including price history, seasonality, promotions, and lags. Handles 47K+ observations efficiently.
  • Price Optimization: Gradient-based and grid search algorithms to maximize revenue or profit subject to business constraints (min margin, max discount).
  • Markdown Strategy: Intelligent clearance pricing for end-of-life products with time-based discount schedules, holding cost calculations, and salvage value optimization.
  • Competitive Analysis: Monitor competitor pricing patterns, identify market positioning opportunities, and recommend competitive strategies.
  • Production-Ready: Comprehensive test suite (94 tests), configuration-driven design, logging, error handling, and CI/CD ready.
